Academic Projects with Teaching Components

Core Team Member and Presenter

Teaching Philosophy

I believe effective economics education bridges theory with real-world applications. My teaching approach emphasizes:

  • Practical Application: Connecting abstract economic concepts to current events and everyday decisions
  • Data Literacy: Developing students' ability to analyze and interpret economic data using appropriate tools
  • Critical Thinking: Encouraging students to question assumptions and evaluate economic arguments from multiple perspectives
  • Inclusive Learning: Creating an environment where diverse viewpoints and learning styles are respected and accommodated
